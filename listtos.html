Add or Remove Links

To Add or Remove links email admin@cinema-blue.icu

Requirements

At the very least, add the following to the room settings MOTD:

  • On the top banner, click "Channel Settings->Edit->MOTD"
  • Put the name of the room followed by "18+"
  • Click "Save MOTD" to comply with CyTu.be "Terms of Use"
  • Under "Channel Settings->Admin Settings"
  • Add the Room Name to "Page title" followed by "18+"

Make it Pretty

If you would like a room header that looks nicer,
Copy and Paste the template below to make the MOTD room header pretty.
Replace {ROOM-NAME} with whatever you want to call the room but include "18+".
A list of colors can be found here: CSS Color Names

    <span style="color: DeepSkyBlue; font-size: 175%; font-weight: 500">{ROOM-NAME} 18+</span><br />
    <span style="color: Yellow">ALL open chat and links must be legal (18+) or you will be <strong>BANNED!</strong></span><br />
